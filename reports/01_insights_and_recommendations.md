# Insights & Recommendations (in my voice)

I don’t like dashboards that only describe what happened. I want the analysis to answer: **so what do we do next?**

## 1) Viral isn’t linear — it comes in waves
The stunt moment created a spike, then a drop, then a second wave when the trailer content rolled out.
That’s why you need a funnel view, not just a “views” chart.

## 2) TikTok did what TikTok does
It dominated reach and sharing. But conversion efficiency depended on whether we gave people a clear next step.
In real life, I’d treat TikTok as awareness + social proof, and judge it on **incremental lift**, not only CPA.

## 3) Owned channels convert best (and that’s not a surprise)
The Website converted better because it’s where intent becomes action.
That doesn’t mean the Website is the best “marketing channel” — it means it’s where you cash the cheque after other channels create demand.

## 4) Wellness behaviour has lag
You won’t see gym visits jump instantly. You see:
- interest first,
- then signups,
- then actual behaviour days later.

## Action plan
- TikTok for attention + proof.
- YouTube for trust + narrative.
- Website for conversion (single CTA, fast page, clear offer).
- Rewards / partner gym offers as accelerators.
- Track cost per membership + behaviour outcomes, not just clicks.
